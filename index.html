About Tattu Charity

Founded with a vision to transform lives across Uganda, Tattu Charity works directly with communities to address their most pressing needs. Every two months, we organize collection drives and distribute essential resources to those who need them most.

Our approach is holistic - we don't just provide temporary relief, but work on sustainable solutions that empower communities to thrive independently.
